nolhyte

Navigate through the shadows and avoid deadly light sources in this thrilling stealth game.

Stay hidden, survive, and complete all 5 levels to win!

Quick Controls

Mouse - Move player

M - Toggle sound

P - Performance metrics

S - Shadow quality

FPS: 0
Frame Time: 0ms

How to Play

Move with your mouse to avoid the light.

Stay in the shadows to survive and earn points.

Complete each level by surviving until the timer runs out.

If you stay in the light too long, your danger meter will fill and you'll lose!
